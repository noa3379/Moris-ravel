Ravel has composed only about 100 songs in his 50-year musical life. In other words, it is a masterpiece. He is said to have a strict personality and a tendency to perfectionism, so he never released the song until he was satisfied. However, most of the songs he composed are immortal masterpieces and songs that are located in a very important place in music history today. In addition to the colors of impressionism, there are many songs that have enhanced the performance effect by using elaborate musical structures and phrases in the song.

Among the songs composed, piano music is a high proportion, and Ravel's piano music is one of the most difficult known piano s ongs. It is famous for demanding high-level techniques and deep musicality that are difficult to perform even for virtuoso pianists.
